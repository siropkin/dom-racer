# DOM Racer — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 17, 2026

## Summary

DOM Racer collects **no personal data** and makes **no network requests**. Everything stays on your device.

## What data is stored

DOM Racer uses `chrome.storage.local` to save:

- Sound preference (on/off)
- Vehicle selection (coupe, buggy, or truck)
- Game scores and run history (per-page bests, lifetime totals)

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

## What the extension accesses

DOM Racer runs as a content script on the active page when you press the hotkey. It reads the positions and types of visible HTML elements (links, buttons, images, form controls) to generate game geometry. No page content is collected, recorded, or transmitted.

## Third-party services

None. DOM Racer makes zero network requests. There are no analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or external dependencies.

## Data sharing

DOM Racer does not sell, transfer, or share any user data with third parties for any purpose.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at https://github.com/siropkin/dom-racer/issues.
