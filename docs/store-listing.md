## Description (for store listing)

DOM Racer turns any webpage into a tiny arcade track.

Press Alt+G on any page and links become coins, text becomes slow zones, images become ice, and form fields sprout weeds. Every page plays differently — a docs page is a slow-zone maze, a dashboard is an open racetrack, a landing page is a slippery ice rink.

GAMEPLAY
• Collect gold coins scattered across the page for score
• Grab power-ups: BONUS, MAGNET, GHOST, INVERT, BLUR, OIL SLICK, REVERSE, MYSTERY, and the rare golden JACKPOT
• Dodge police chases with flashing sirens and a proper GAME OVER screen
• Watch for airplane flyovers that drop bonus coins, specials, and lucky wind
• Survive the overgrowth — grass sprouts from obstacles, grows into bushes, then trees, narrowing your routes
• Thread near-misses against walls and police for a satisfying visual flash (CLOSE! TIGHT! RAZOR!)
• Complete micro-objectives: collect N coins in X seconds for +20/+30/+40 bonus points

DAILY VARIETY
Each day brings a different rule twist — double coins, fast police, extra specials, slippery surfaces, or early overgrowth. Come back tomorrow for a new challenge.

PROGRESSION
• Track your run count, page bests, and lifetime score
• Unlock the Buggy and Truck by hitting score milestones
• Hit lifetime milestones for surprise celebration toasts

PRIVACY
No data collection. No analytics. No network requests. Everything stays on your device.

Controls: WASD/arrows to drive, Space for nitro, R to restart, V to switch vehicle, M for sound, Esc to quit. Press Alt+G to start.
